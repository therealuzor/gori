A warm welcome

Griggs Specialist Hospital is an American Hospital Standard, full service medical facility located in Lagos, Nigeria. We are staffed by doctors and other personnel recruited from the United States and other countries and working with dedicated Nigerian counterparts to provide exceptional medical services to our patients. The patient always comes first, and we constantly strive to exceed each individual patient's expectations. Our attention to quality and service has made us an industry leader in Nigeria.
